Finalist

Thru the chair

Thru the chair is an initiative founded with the ideology of contributing significantly towards solving the current educational challenges in Southern Africa. The initiative aims to expose 1000 unique individuals by telling 1000 visual stories through 1000 reinvented chairs which will be auctioned to Chair of boards resulting in 1000 opportunities to make a difference.
